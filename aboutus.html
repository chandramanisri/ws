MATA RAMA DEVI TRUST is a National Public Non Governmental Organisation with nation wide operations dedicated to EDUCATION FOR POOR CHILDRENS, AND DEVELOPMENT IN THE SECTORS OF SLUMS

We is integrated into a national network of customers, clients and partners. Its major partners in cooperation besides the Corporate sectors consist of more than 500 EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS among the Schools, Colleges and Universities.

We offer instruction on inter disciplinary management techniques and impart international and inter cultural know-how. In addition, we provide the opportunity to discuss potential solution to operational problems in an informal setting.

We creates networks that support nationwide learning. After the training programmes, our participants can continue to participate in the exchange of knowledge through interdisciplinary and trans-regional alumni networks. Our learning platform assists participants with the practical application as well as expansion of their skills.
